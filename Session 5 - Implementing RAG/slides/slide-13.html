RAG vs Fine-Tuning

RAG

No training needed - plug in your data

Dynamic data - update knowledge base anytime

Easy to update - add or remove documents

Cost effective - no GPU training required

Use when: Data changes frequently

Fine-Tuning

Requires training on curated datasets

Static knowledge - baked into model weights

Expensive - needs GPU compute for training

Deeper customisation of model behaviour

Use when: You need behaviour change
