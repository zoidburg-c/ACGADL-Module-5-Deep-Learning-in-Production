Key Takeaways

  • Quantisation is the easiest win — often 2-4x improvement with minimal accuracy loss
  • Pruning removes unnecessary weights — good for already-trained models
  • Distillation creates purpose-built smaller models — best for new deployments
  • These techniques can be combined for even greater gains
