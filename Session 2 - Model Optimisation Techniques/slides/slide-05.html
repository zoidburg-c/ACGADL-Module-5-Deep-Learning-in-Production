Types of Quantisation

1

Dynamic Quantisation

Weights are quantised ahead of time. Activations are quantised dynamically at inference time. No calibration data needed.

Easiest to apply

2

Static Quantisation

Both weights and activations are pre-quantised. Requires a representative calibration dataset to determine optimal scale factors.

Better accuracy

3

Quantisation-Aware Training

Quantisation effects are simulated during training. The model learns to compensate for reduced precision, yielding best results.

Best accuracy
